How We Use Cookies

Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how eBathtub.com uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our Site, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember preferences, and — in the case of affiliate marketing — track which referral led to a purchase so commissions can be properly attributed.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for core site functionality, such as remembering your filter selections while browsing the shop page. The Site cannot function properly without these.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

Set when you click a "Shop Now" or affiliate link. These cookies (typically lasting up to 90 days) allow the relevant retailer or affiliate network to attribute a resulting purchase back to eBathtub.com or one of our affiliates.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use the Site — which pages are popular, how long visitors stay, and where they came from — so we can improve content and product selection.

Preference Cookies

Remember choices you've made, such as wishlist items, so you don't have to re-select them on every visit.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties we work with, including affiliate networks (e.g., Amazon Associates, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate), our form processor (Formspree), and any analytics tools we use. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies governing their use of this data.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect Site functionality, and disabling affiliate tracking cookies means a retailer may not be able to attribute your purchase to eBathtub.com.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Check back for the latest version, indicated by the "Last updated" date above.
